Future Outlook

The market is evolving fast. Here are the key trends shaping the future of AI-driven development.

The Rise of the AI Orchestrator

The developer's role is shifting from writing code to architecting systems and guiding AI agents, making high-level product vision more valuable than ever.

Squeeze on Low-End Web Development

AI tools that build simple sites in minutes will pressure the market for template-based freelance work, pushing developers towards more complex, high-value tasks.

Open-Source & "Bring Your Own AI"

Open-source solutions like `bolt.diy` signal a move towards platforms where users can plug in their own LLM API keys, offering more control and reducing vendor lock-in.

Convergence and Consolidation

The lines between AI-native generators and traditional no-code platforms will blur as they adopt each other's features, leading to a more competitive and consolidated market.
